Unlock Your Leader DNA

Welcome, Rising Stars! Get ready for an epic adventure to unlock the leadership potential God has placed inside you. This isn't your average training. We're talking high-energy, "aha!" moments, and real-talk strategies to help you change the game.

Let's Go!

Week 1: The Heartbeat

Igniting the Fire: Cultivating Passion

Passion is the unshakeable *why* that fuels everything you do. Like Jesus in Mark 2, a genuine passion for people and purpose draws others in. It's not just something you're born with; it's something you **cultivate** by remembering your divine calling and serving with joy.

What's in the Mix for Passion?

Enthusiasm:

The contagious spark that makes people lean in.

Drive:

The inner engine that keeps you moving, even when it's tough.

Commitment:

Sticking with it, through thick and thin. It's loyalty to the vision.

Inspiration:

Being a source of energy and vision for yourself and others.

Dedication:

Pouring your whole self into the mission.

Joy & Engagement:

Finding genuine delight and being fully present in the work.

Week 2: The Foundation

Building Rock-Solid Foundations: Cultivating Integrity

After igniting passion, we lay down the foundation of Integrity. It's about being the same person in every situation—reliable, honest, and trustworthy. This commitment to character builds the platform for breakthrough.

What's in the Mix for Integrity?

Honesty:

Telling the truth, even when it's tough.

Trustworthiness:

Being someone others can consistently rely on.

Ethics:

Doing what's right, even when no one is watching.

Accountability:

Owning your actions and responsibilities.

Transparency:

Being open and clear in your dealings.

Consistency:

Being reliable and predictable in your character and actions.

Fairness:

Treating everyone justly and equitably.

Week 3: The Impact

Sowing for an Epic Harvest: Cultivating Fruitfulness

With passion and integrity, it's time to make it count. Fruitfulness isn't about being busy; it's about making a real, tangible impact. When we remain connected to the Vine, we bear much fruit.

What's in the Mix for Fruitfulness?

Results-oriented:

Focused on achieving clear, measurable outcomes.

Impact:

Making a tangible, positive difference.

Productivity:

Getting meaningful work done efficiently.

Effectiveness:

Doing the right things to achieve the desired effect.

Growth-minded:

Always looking for ways to expand, improve, and level up.

Innovation:

Finding new, creative, and better ways to do things.

Contribution:

Adding real value to the team and the mission.

Week 4: The Drive

The Go-Getter Vibe: Cultivating Hustle

This is the final sprint! Hustle is that proactive, persistent, "get-it-done" energy that turns vision into reality. It's the "extra mile" spirit that brings breakthrough, just like the friends in Mark 2.

What's in the Mix for Hustle?

Proactiveness:

Taking initiative, not waiting to be told.

Diligence:

Consistently putting in the hard work, paying attention to detail.

Initiative:

Being the one to start things, to jump in.

Persistence:

Not giving up when things get tough; pushing through obstacles.

Work Ethic:

A strong commitment to putting in the effort required.

Agility:

Being able to quickly adapt and pivot when plans change.

Resourcefulness:

Finding creative ways to solve problems with what you have.
